About

Art comes in many different forms — each with its own creativity, ideas, and soul. Over the years, I’ve explored a few of them: picking up hobbies, trying new things, and diving into different creative outlets like painting, playing, and even producing music or sounds that helped me express what words couldn’t.

Now, I find that same spark in coding. It challenges me, pushes me to learn, and extends my creativity through design and interaction. Seeing various websites or portfolios with fascinating interfaces made by professional designers and developers inspires and motivates me. This portfolio, for example, was inspired by an Italian developer Patrick David.

Personally, detail matters a lot. I admire creators who take time implementing even the smallest touches, whether it's in visual design, music, gaming, or anything else. Even the smallest detail can make things truly stand out.

I'm still learning, like most of us — experimenting with new things, tools, and figuring out what works. I'm excited to create more meaningful and interesting designs in the future and share them with the world as I go, one step at a time.
